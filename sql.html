Data cleaning, processing, analysis and data bases with SQL

SQL was part of the analysis' framework for the global online and retail supermarket project covered in the Tableau section of this portfolio. I cleaned, processed, analysed the market dataset, and created databases in PostgreSQL to speed-up quick intermediary insights.

A global online and retail supermarket requested an assessment of customers' purchasing behaviour to plan a future marketing campaign. It is an exploratory and diagnostic analysis of purchasing trends by:

  • marketplace (country, online/in-store, products),
  • customer sociodemographics (age, family status, education, income),
  • and advertising channels (mail/social media).
